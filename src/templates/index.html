Another World Cup, another occasion to pit cloudreacher against cloudreacher for the title of the ultimate football clairvoyant! Think that person is you? If so, join us in the Cloudreach World Cup 2018 predictions game!

The game will run two concurrent competitions:

The sweepstake competition

No skills are involved here. Each participant will be allocated one of the 32 countries in the tournament. You win if your team wins the tournament! However, there's a twist... As we will likely have more than 32 people participate, you might have to share the glory of winning with other people. The game will ensure all teams have been allocated at least once before making them available again for a further round of allocations.

The score prediction competition

Are you the next Paul the Octopus? In this competition, you will be required to predict the scores for each game of the World Cup. You will:

  • Earn 1 point for each game where you correctly predict a draw or the winning team but got the exact score wrong
  • Earn 3 points for each game where you correctly predict the exact score
